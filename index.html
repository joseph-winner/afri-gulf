AfriGulf is a Qatar-based firm dedicated to fostering transformative partnerships between Africa and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC), with a special focus on Qatar. Our mission is to unlock economic and cultural opportunities through innovative solutions in corporate training, tourism development, and business facilitation. By bridging these dynamic regions, we empower organizations, entrepreneurs, and communities to thrive in a globalized world. Inspired by Qatar’s National Vision 2030 and Africa’s Agenda 2063, AfriGulf combines local expertise with global insights to drive sustainable growth and cross-cultural collaboration.

Featured Services

What We Offer For You

Corporate Training

We design tailored training programs to enhance skills in policy development, digital innovation, and creative industries. Our workshops empower African and GCC organizations to excel in competitive global markets, fostering leadership and innovation.

Tourism Development

From cultural heritage tours to student tourism programs, we create immersive experiences that showcase the best of Africa and Qatar. Our initiatives promote sustainable tourism and cross-cultural exchange, boosting local economies.

Business Facilitation

We connect African entrepreneurs with GCC markets, offering support in market entry, partnerships, and investment opportunities. Our services include startup incubation, trade missions, and strategic collaborations to drive economic growth.

Student Tourism

Our student tourism programs offer transformative educational and cultural experiences, connecting African and GCC youth. From heritage tours in Qatar’s Souq Waqif to innovation workshops at Education City, we inspire the next generation through immersive learning journeys.

Sports Tourism

We curate thrilling sports tourism experiences, connecting African visitors to Qatar’s world-class sporting events, such as the FIFA U-17 World Cup and Qatar Grand Prix. Our packages include stadium tours, sports workshops, and cultural excursions, fostering sports-driven cultural exchange.

Events Management

Our expert team delivers seamless event planning and execution for corporate, cultural, and sporting events in Qatar. From logistics to promotion, we ensure unforgettable experiences, leveraging Qatar’s state-of-the-art venues like Khalifa International Stadium.
